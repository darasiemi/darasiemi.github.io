Who is the greatest inventor?

The world is really blessed with many great inventors. Giving an undisputable answer to this question is therefore almost impossible. Nevertheless, a few of them deserve the accolade; one of which is Thomas Alva Edison. Thomas Edison was an American inventor and businessman with 1093 patents to his name. 1093? Wow. That is just…Wow!

He contributed to the incandescent light bulb, phonograph, electrical systems, motion picture camera, telegraph, telephone, X-ray…you seriously shouldn’t expect me to list a thousand things here.
