The stage is electrifying; the audience is intrigued; swords of words are screeching in the arena. Jaw War is here again! Jaw War, the biggest and most anticipated public speaking and oratory contest in the University of Ibadan set sail on Wednesday, 14th of September, 2016, with the first round of debates for the Halls of Residence. The theme for this year’s contest is Nigeria;Africa’s heartbeat in Coma. The defending champion and one of the favourites of the contest-the Faculty of Social Sciences- was pitched with the Faculty of Technology in the second phase of the debate, in the clash of the Faculties. The topic for discuss was Bicameral or Unicameral: The best system of legislation?
